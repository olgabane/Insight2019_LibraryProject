ABOUT OLGA







Hello! My name is Olga Minkina. I created BiblioPal as an Insight Data Science Fellow in January of 2019. I got my Ph.D. in molecular biology at Harvard University where I generated and analyzed genomic data to understand how information is transmitted across generations. As a postdoc at New York University, I applied clustering algorithms to highly-dimensional genomic data to study how neurons move through the developing brain.
